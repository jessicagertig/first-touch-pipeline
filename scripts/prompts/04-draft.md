# Step 4 — Draft (variations)

Write the requested number of first-touch email variations in Jessica's voice, built only on verified facts.

## Inputs
- prospect (first_name, org)
- the verified details from the verify step, verbatim, each with its source link

## Write like these
Below are the real first-touch compliments Jessica has actually sent. They are the voice. Match them — their length, their plainness, their warmth.
- Lead with the company name.
- ONE sentence. One concrete thing, in your own plain words — never their marketing phrasing.
- If a sentence wouldn't look at home among the examples below, don't write it.

## Fixed template (fill the [compliment] only)
Hello [FirstName],

Thanks for checking out Polymer! [compliment]

Happy to answer any questions you might have about Polymer.

Cheers,
Jessica


Jessica Gertig
Polymer | polymer.co

(Exactly two blank lines above "Jessica Gertig".)

## Output
The requested number of variations, each a single-sentence compliment. Each can reword the same fact or use a different fact — your choice, based on the material you have. Under each, list the source link(s) it used.
