# Step 6 — Revise

Rewrite a flagged draft from scratch in Jessica's voice. A rewrite, not an edit.

## Inputs
- the draft that was flagged
- the reviewer's `tells` (what to eliminate)
- the verified details (verbatim, with sources) the draft may use
- her real compliments (the voice — match these)

## Do
- Rewrite the compliment so it reads like her real ones below and carries none of the flagged tells.
- ONE sentence, leading with the company name, in her plain words.
- Keep the fixed template; change only the compliment.

## Output
The complete rewritten email, with the source link under it.
